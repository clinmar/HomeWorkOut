Bicep Curls

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand.
Keep your elbows close to your body and curl the weights up towards your shoulders.
Lower them back down slowly.

Tricep Dips


Sit on the edge of a sturdy bench or chair with your hands next to your hips.
Slide your butt off the edge, supporting your weight with your hands.
Lower your body by bending your elbows, then push back up.

Push-Ups


Start in a plank position with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the ground.
Push back up to the starting position.

Hammer Curls


Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand with palms facing your body.
Curl the weights up, keeping your palms facing each other.
Lower them back down slowly.

Overhead Tricep Extension


Stand or sit with a dumbbell held in both hands above your head.
Lower the weight behind your head by bending your elbows.
Extend your arms back up to the starting position.

Lateral Raises

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand at your sides.
Raise your arms out to the sides until they are level with your shoulders.

Close-Grip Bench Press


Lie on a bench holding a barbell with a grip narrower than shoulder-width.
Lower the barbell to your chest, keeping your elbows close to your body.

Concentration Curls


Sit on a bench with your legs spread.
Hold a dumbbell in one hand, resting your elbow on the inside of your thigh.

Skull Crushers:


Lie on a bench holding a barbell with hands shoulder-width apart.
Extend your arms straight up.
Lower the barbell towards your forehead by bending your elbows.
Extend your arms back to the starting position.

Arnold Press:


Sit or stand with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing your body.
Press the weights overhead while rotating your palms to face forward.
Reverse the motion as you lower the weights back to the starting position.
