Arm Circles

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and extend your arms straight out to the sides. Rotate your arms in small circles, gradually increasing the size of the circles. Do this for 30 seconds .

Leg Lifts

Sit on the edge of a chair with your feet flat on the ground. Lift one leg off the ground, extending it straight out in front of you. Hold for a few seconds, then lower it back down. Repeat with the other leg. Aim for 3osecond on each leg.

Ankle Flexes

Sit on a chair with your feet flat on the ground. Lift one foot off the ground and flex your ankle, pointing your toes up toward the ceiling. Hold for a few seconds, then relax. Repeat with the other foot. Do 10-15 repetitions on each foot.

Toe Taps

Sit on a chair with your feet flat on the ground. Lift one foot off the ground and tap your toes on the floor in front of you. Repeat with the other foot. Aim for 10-15 taps on each foot.

Shoulder Rolls:

Stand tall with your arms relaxed at your sides. Roll your shoulders forward in a circular motion, then reverse the direction and roll them backward. Do this for 30 seconds to 1 minute.

Calf Raises

Stand with your feet hip-width apart and raise up onto the balls of your feet, lifting your heels off the ground. Hold for a moment, then lower your heels back down. Aim for 10-15 repetitions.

Wrist Flexes

Extend your arms out in front of you with your palms facing down. Flex your wrists upward, pointing your fingers toward the ceiling. Hold for a few seconds, then relax. Repeat 10-15 times.

Knee Lifts

Stand tall with your feet hip-width apart. Lift one knee up towards your chest, then lower it back down. Repeat on the other side. Aim for 10-15 lifts on each side.

Side Bends:

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and arms at your sides. Slowly lean to one side, sliding your hand down your leg towards your knee. Hold for a few seconds, then return to the starting position. Repeat on the other side. Aim for 10-15 bends on each side.

Hip Circles

Stand with your feet hip-width apart and hands on your hips. Circle your hips clockwise, then reverse the direction and circle them counterclockwise. Do this for 30 seconds .
